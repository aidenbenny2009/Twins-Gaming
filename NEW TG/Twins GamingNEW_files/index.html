CODE OF CONDUCT

Clan Rules

Everyone owners included follows these. Only owners can edit this list.

🤝

Respect Everyone

No harassment, hate speech, or toxicity toward teammates or opponents. Win or lose, represent the crest well.

🎮

Show Up

If you sign up for a scrim, practice, or event, attend or notify the team in advance. Repeated no-shows affect your role.

🔒

No Cheating

Zero tolerance for cheats, exploits, or smurfing in ranked play. Immediate removal, no exceptions.

💬

Use the Right Channels

Keep Discord organized. Announcements stay in announcements, banter stays in general. Admins may move misplaced messages.

🛡️

Chain of Command

Disputes go to a Moderator first, then to an Administrator, then to an Owner if unresolved. Owner decisions are final but always explained.

📵

No Spam or Self-Promo

Do not advertise other clans, servers, or products without permission from an Owner.
